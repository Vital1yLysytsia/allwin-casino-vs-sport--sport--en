Football betting – a recommended option for beginners in the world of betting.

Initially, football betting was one of the forms of entertainment. As an example, we can mention pari-mutuel - a long-existing pool system that originated in Britain at the beginning of the 20th century with the following conditions:

  • Outside the stadiums, fans bought tickets at a minimal price (later tickets began to be distributed through newspapers, which were cut out);
  • Fans entered into cards those results of future matches of "the number one game" that, in their opinion, were more likely;
  • Coupons were handed over to a pool collection agent or sent to the organizing company by mail.

The specialized office guaranteed that there would be at least one winner during the week. The lucky ones were determined by the number of points scored among all fans who sent cards.

The pari-mutuel system allowed earning money by making the first football bets with large sums. For example, Michael Elliott, risking only 2 pounds sterling, earned more than 3 million pounds sterling. He managed to correctly predict 8 matches that would end in a draw with a score of 2:2.

The pool system was successful until "bookmakers" began to appear in the form in which they exist now. Fans of "the number one game" got the opportunity to place bets with great comfort.
